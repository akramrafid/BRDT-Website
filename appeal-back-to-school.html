Back to School

Every child deserves the tools to learn. Help BRDT equip underprivileged students in rural Bangladesh with the books, stationery and supplies they need to build a brighter future.

Home/Appeals/Back to School

Education is a Right, Not a Privilege

In rural Bangladesh, thousands of children begin each academic year without the basic supplies they need to learn effectively. Families living in poverty are forced to choose between food and education materials — and education often loses. Without books, pens, and bags, children fall behind, lose confidence, and often drop out of school entirely.

BRDT's Back to School appeal addresses this crisis head-on. Each year, BRDT distributes comprehensive education packs to underprivileged students across Belghar — from Kindergarten children starting their learning journey all the way through to university students pursuing higher education. The goal is simple: no child should be left behind because their family cannot afford a pen.

Education transforms futures: Research shows that every additional year of schooling increases a child's future earnings by 8–10%. When you help a child stay in school by providing their supplies, you are not just buying a book — you are investing in a lifetime of opportunity for them and their family.

What's Included in Each Education Pack

  • Textbooks and exercise notebooks for the full academic year
  • Pens, pencils, rulers, erasers and geometry sets
  • School bags (shoulder bags or backpacks by level)
  • Calculators for secondary school and college students
  • Exam registration fee assistance for SSC and HSC students

BRDT works directly with local schools and community leaders to identify the most needy students, ensuring supplies reach genuine beneficiaries — not duplicated or misused. Packs are tailored to education level, from simple colouring books for young children to full stationery sets for university students.

Your Donation in Action

See exactly how your Back to School donation makes an impact in Belghar's classrooms.

Identify & Reach the Neediest

BRDT works with local school teachers and community leaders to identify the most underprivileged students before each academic year. We ensure that packs go to children from families who genuinely cannot afford education supplies — prioritising orphans and children from single-parent homes.

Pack & Distribute

Volunteers assemble tailored education packs based on each child's school level. Distribution events are held at local schools and community centres across Belghar, creating a celebration of learning that motivates children and brings communities together with pride and hope.

Improve Attendance & Results

Children who receive education packs through BRDT show measurably better school attendance and academic performance. Equipped and supported, they arrive at school with confidence — ready to learn, participate, and achieve the results that will shape their futures.

Frequently Asked Questions

Learn more about BRDT's Back to School appeal and how it helps children in Belghar.

Which students benefit from the Back to School appeal?

BRDT supports students from Kindergarten through to university level. Priority is given to children from families living below the poverty line, orphans, children of widows, and those who have previously had to drop out due to lack of supplies. Packs are customised for each educational level.

When are education packs distributed?

Packs are typically distributed at the start of the academic year in Bangladesh, which runs from January. BRDT also distributes mid-year when funds allow, to support students who join the programme later or whose supplies run out. Donations are accepted year-round.

How much does it cost to fund one student's pack?

A primary school education pack costs approximately ৳300–500. A secondary school pack with stationery, textbooks, and a geometry set costs ৳500–1,000. A university-level pack including financial assistance for exam fees can cost ৳1,000–2,000. Any contribution, however small, makes a difference.

Does BRDT provide financial assistance for exam fees?

Yes. In addition to physical supplies, BRDT provides financial assistance to students who cannot afford to pay exam registration fees for major national exams like the SSC (Secondary School Certificate) and HSC (Higher Secondary Certificate). Missing these exams due to financial reasons can permanently derail a student's future.
