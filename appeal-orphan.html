Be a Guardian for an Orphan

Bangladesh is home to millions of orphaned children — children who have lost one or both parents to illness, accidents, poverty, or natural disasters. Without a father's income or a mother's care, these children are among the most vulnerable members of rural communities, often falling into extreme poverty, malnutrition, and dropping out of school.

BRDT's Orphan Sponsorship programme provides vulnerable children in Belghar with the consistent monthly support they need to survive and thrive. Your sponsorship covers their essential needs — food, education, healthcare, and clothing — giving them the foundation to grow up with dignity, security, and hope for a better future.

The Prophet Muhammad ﷺ said: "I and the one who cares for an orphan will be like these two in Paradise" — and he pointed with his index and middle fingers, holding them close together. — Sahih Bukhari. No act of charity brings you closer to the Prophet ﷺ than caring for an orphan.

What Your Sponsorship Provides Monthly

  • Nutritious daily meals and food security for the child
  • School enrolment, supplies, and tuition fees
  • Healthcare access — medical check-ups and medicines
  • Seasonal clothing appropriate for Bangladesh's climate
  • Psychological support and community integration programmes

BRDT's orphan welfare programme also includes support for widowed mothers — because when we care for the family unit, we give the child the best possible environment to flourish. The entire family benefits when an orphan is sponsored, not just the child.

How Sponsorship Works

A transparent, effective monthly programme that transforms the lives of orphaned children in Bangladesh.

Monthly Care

Your monthly sponsorship provides consistent, reliable support that an orphaned child can depend on. Unlike one-off donations, a monthly commitment allows BRDT to plan ahead — ensuring the child never goes a day without food, education, or healthcare throughout the year.

Education & Future

A sponsored orphan remains in school, receives their supplies and fees, and has access to tutoring and mentoring support from BRDT's team. Education is the single most powerful tool we have to break the cycle of poverty — and your sponsorship makes it possible for one child at a time.

Spiritual Reward

Caring for an orphan is described by the Prophet ﷺ as one of the highest acts a believer can perform. Your monthly sponsorship earns ongoing reward — Sadaqah Jariyah — every single month. It is an investment in your Akhirah and a direct connection to the Sunnah of the Prophet ﷺ.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about sponsoring an orphan through BRDT.

Who qualifies as an orphan in BRDT's programme?

In Islamic terminology, an orphan (Yateem) is a child who has lost their father, as the father is traditionally the family's provider. BRDT also supports children who have lost both parents. All children in the programme come from verified, low-income families in the Belghar region of Bangladesh who have no other means of consistent financial support.

Will I receive updates about my sponsored orphan?

BRDT provides periodic programme reports to sponsors, including updates on school progress, health assessments, and general welfare of children in the programme. We protect the privacy and dignity of all children and their families, so individual identifying details are shared carefully and respectfully.

Can I stop my monthly sponsorship at any time?

Yes. You can cancel your monthly sponsorship at any time by contacting BRDT. However, we encourage sponsors to commit for as long as possible, as consistency makes the greatest difference to a child's development. If you need to pause, BRDT will try to find another sponsor to bridge the gap.

Is orphan sponsorship a form of Sadaqah Jariyah?

Yes, absolutely. By funding a child's education through sponsorship, you are contributing to beneficial knowledge — one of the three deeds that continue after death. Every prayer the sponsored child makes because of the Islamic education enabled by your support is a form of ongoing reward that flows back to you.
