Our Institution

A Vital Role in Shaping the Future

Belghar Rural Development Trust is already playing a vital role in shaping the future of underprivileged children by renovating and running Belghar Madinatul Ulum Madrasa and Orphanage. Through this institution, the Trust provides a modern, age-appropriate, safe, caring and well-organized environment where children receive both academic and moral education.

The Madrasa imparts quality education rooted in cultural and spiritual values, which will ensure that students grow up with strong character, discipline and a sense of purpose.

The Orphanage

Compassion & Holistic Development

The orphanage, run with the same compassionate approach, provides shelter, food, healthcare and psychological support to children who have lost their parents’ affection. The Trust ensures that every child is treated with dignity and is given the opportunity to learn, grow and dream.

Beyond basic needs, the organization focuses on holistic development - encouraging participation in extra-curricular activities, sports, travel, competitive skill-building programs and social engagement.

  • Shelter & Food

  • Extra-curriculars

"Belghar Rural Development Trust, through its dedication and integrity in running Belghar Madinatul Ulum Madrasa and Orphanage, continues to transform the lives of children."

Its efforts not only lead to the personal development of children, but also strengthen the social fabric of the entire upazila and install hope and opportunity where it is needed most.
