WE PROSPER THE HOUSE OF ALLAH

WHAT WE PROVIDE

Suhur & Iftar

Providing essential nutritious meals to needy families so they can observe the fast with dignity.

Semai & Sugar

Distributing traditional food items to ensure no family is left out of Eid celebrations.

Zakat Fund

Collecting and distributing Zakat accurately to the most deserving and underprivileged individuals.

Korbani Meat

Delivering sacrificial meat to the homes of those who are unable to sacrifice on Eid al-Adha.
