President’s Message

It is with deep gratitude and pride that I share this message on behalf of BRDT. Over the past years, we have worked with sincerity, transparency, and a shared commitment to uplift our community. Together, we have honoured the trust placed in us and continued to expand the impact of our mission.

From the very beginning, our goal has been simple yet profound — to stand beside people, to listen to their needs, and to create meaningful opportunities for progress. Through the collective effort of our members, volunteers, and well-wishers, BRDT has grown into a dependable platform for social development.

During the last two terms, we have carried out a wide range of humanitarian and development initiatives. These include emergency relief, education support, health assistance, community development, and direct financial aid to vulnerable families. Every project reflects our belief that service to people is the highest form of responsibility.

Who We Are

Belghar Rural Development Trust (BRDT) is a community‑driven humanitarian organisation dedicated to uplifting underprivileged families with dignity, compassion, and long‑term vision. We work across education, healthcare, social welfare, environmental protection, and cultural development to ensure that no one in our community is left behind.

We support students through mentoring, books, and essential learning materials. Our initiatives extend to every stage of life — from providing warm clothes in winter and food support during Ramadan and Eid, to delivering sacrificial meat to families who cannot afford it.

BRDT also responds swiftly during disasters, runs free medical camps, supports poor families during marriages, plants trees, promotes cleanliness, and prepares disadvantaged youth for employment. We proudly manage Belghar Madinatul Uloom Madrasa and Orphanage, where “every child is treated with dignity and given the opportunity to learn, grow and dream.”

At BRDT, we believe in unity, service, and shared responsibility. Together with volunteers, donors, and community members, we strive to build a peaceful, educated, and empowered society — one act of kindness at a time.

Our Mission

Our mission is to uplift underprivileged communities through education, compassion, and sustainable development. We work to ensure that every child learns with confidence, every family lives with dignity, and every individual has access to opportunities that help them grow. Guided by the belief that “people come to BRDT whenever they have a problem,” we stand beside the community in education, healthcare, social welfare, disaster relief, and cultural preservation.

We strive to serve with sincerity, empower with knowledge, and support with humanity.

BRDT's Values

BRDT believes that standing by people is the most beautiful form of humanity. By working with different communities, we want to make the lives of people in need a little safer, more respectful and brighter.

Compassion

Building relationships of kindness, understanding and love is our strength.

Dignity

Every person is valuable. We respect them, help them awaken their strengths.

Excellence

We improve ourselves every day to maintain the best standards in everything we do.

Justice

Ensuring fair opportunities for all is at the heart of our work.

Our faith teaches us that no matter their religion, race, colour, age or ability, everyone is part of our family. We know that poverty is a complex reality. That is why we want to join hands with communities to address its deep structural causes.

We are ready to partner with everyone on this journey - regardless of their beliefs, ideologies, or identities.
