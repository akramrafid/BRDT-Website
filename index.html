Our Work

Provides mentoring and overall support to students

Belghar Rural Development Trust (BRDT) plays a vital role in strengthening the educational foundation of students from underprivileged communities. The organization provides structured mentoring and ongoing support to young learners in both reading and writing, which helps build confidence and skills.

Through individualized guidance, students receive mentoring sessions to meet their individual learning needs. This approach ensures that they do not fall behind, but are encouraged to progress at their own pace. The Trust's mentors often act not only as teachers but also as role models, creating a positive learning environment.

Overall, BRDT provides essential books, notebooks, pens, calculators, geometry boxes and shoulder bags to students from Kinder Garden to Class V, Class VI to Secondary School Certificate (SSC), From college to university level. We also provide financial assistance to those who need money to fill up forms.
